Shipping Policy

1. Global Shipping

We are proud to offer worldwide shipping. For simplicity and transparency, shipping costs are included in the displayed price of the unit. There are no surprise fees added at checkout.

2. Processing & Lead Times

The Bitcoin Indicator Panel is a handmade product. Please note the following timelines:

  • Production: ~7 days from order confirmation.

You will receive an email confirmation when your order is placed, and a second email with tracking information once your unit has been dispatched.

3. Customs & Duties

Please be aware that international shipments may be subject to import taxes, customs duties, and fees levied by the destination country's customs department.

Luko Studio has no control over these charges. The recipient is the importer of record and must comply with all laws and regulations of the destination country. Any additional charges for customs clearance must be fulfilled by the recipient.
