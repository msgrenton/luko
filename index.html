How accurate is the data?

The panel connects via Wi-Fi to our low-latency price servers. It updates in near real-time, syncing seamlessly with market movements.

What happens if Wi-Fi disconnects?

The panel will pulse a soft white light to indicate connection loss. Once Wi-Fi is restored, it automatically resumes normal operation.
