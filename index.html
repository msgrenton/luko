How it works
No cloud dashboard, no account creation, no mobile app zoo. Just your Wi-Fi, one small web page on the controller, and a light on the wall.
1

Power it and join its Wi-Fi

Plug the panel in. It exposes a small Wi-Fi network. Join it once from your phone or laptop and open the built-in setup page.

2

Set your Wi-Fi and symbol

Enter your home or office Wi-Fi credentials and choose what to track: BTCUSD, ETHUSD or another pair. The ESP32 starts pulling prices from an exchange / API.

3

Let the wall tell you first

When the price moves up — the glow goes green. When it goes down — it bleeds red. Subtle enough for a living room, loud enough for a trading desk.
