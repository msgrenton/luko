Refund Policy

1. Returns & Cancellations

Due to the bespoke, handmade nature of our products and the irreversible nature of cryptocurrency transactions, all sales are final. We generally do not accept returns for "change of mind" once an order has been processed.

Order cancellations may be requested within 24 hours of payment, provided production has not yet commenced. A cancellation fee covering network transaction costs may apply.

2. Damaged or Defective Items

We stand by the quality of our engineering. If your unit arrives damaged or exhibits a manufacturing defect:

  • Please contact us at studio@lukostudio.shop within 7 days of delivery.
  • Include your order number and clear photos/videos of the issue.

If the defect is verified, we will arrange for a repair, replacement part, or a replacement unit at no cost to you.

3. Warranty

Every Bitcoin Indicator Panel includes:

  • 12-Month Structural Warranty: Covers the wood core, frame, and physical assembly.
  • 30-Day Electronics Warranty: Covers the logic board, LEDs, and power components.

This warranty does not cover damage caused by misuse, improper installation, or unauthorized modification.
